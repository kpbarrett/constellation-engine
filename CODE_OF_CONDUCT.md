# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Constellation Engine aims to be a serious, mission-driven open-source project. We expect participants to act with respect, honesty, and operational maturity.

Examples of expected behavior:

- critique ideas, artifacts, and evidence rather than attacking people
- disclose uncertainty and correct mistakes directly
- respect contributor time by being specific, reviewable, and accountable
- preserve provenance when humans or AIs contribute

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, intimidation, or discriminatory conduct
- deliberate misrepresentation of evidence, authorship, or review status
- doxxing or disclosure of private information
- disruptive behavior intended to derail discussion rather than improve the project

## Scope

This applies in GitHub issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, and project communication spaces tied to this repository.

## Reporting

For conduct concerns, contact the maintainers privately rather than escalating in public when that would reduce safety or clarity.

For security concerns, use the process in `SECURITY.md`.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, pause discussion, restrict participation, or escalate to stronger action when behavior threatens contributor safety, project integrity, or truthful collaboration.
